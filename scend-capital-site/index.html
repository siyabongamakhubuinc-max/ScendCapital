Our Process

A clear path to returns

Three participant types. One disciplined framework. Every step documented and legally enforceable.

01
FICA & Onboarding
Complete KYC verification — certified ID, proof of address, POPIA consent, Risk Disclosure signature. Under 15 minutes.
02
Review Prospectuses
Receive deal prospectuses (SC-PROS) with business profiles, risk scores, projected returns, and subscription parameters.
03
Execute Agreement
Sign the Commandant Partnership Agreement (SC-CPA) and EFT R 500–R 10,000 using the deal reference number.
04
Monitor Weekly
Receive verified revenue reports every Friday — full visibility on revenue, COGS, and profit for the cycle duration.
05
Receive Distribution
Capital and profit share transferred within 5 business days of close, with a full Settlement Statement (SC-DSS).
01
Business Assessment
A scout visits, verifies 4 weeks of trading history, and scores your business on 7 criteria out of 35.
02
Deal Structuring
Capital amount, duration (4–12 weeks), profit share %, and reporting obligations confirmed in a signed Profit Share Agreement.
03
Capital Disbursement
Capital EFT'd to your account. Proof of expenditure within 3 business days confirms proper deployment.
04
Weekly Reporting
Submit a simple revenue form every Friday. Scend Capital calculates its share — you retain the contracted balance.
05
Close & Scale
Strong operators receive priority access to larger deals and repeat funding rounds. Build your track record.
01
Sign Agreements
Execute the Scout Commission Agreement (SC-DSC) and NDA. Independent contractor on your own schedule.
02
Identify Prospects
Source informal businesses in Gauteng with 4+ weeks of trading and capital needs of R 2,000–R 10,000.
03
Submit Assessment
Complete the DIA Form (SC-DIA) with 7-criterion scoring, site visit photos, and operator identification documents.
04
Earn Commission
Deployment commission on activation + performance bonus on successful close. Paid within 5 business days.
